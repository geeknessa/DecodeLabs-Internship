🍳Cook Time: 15 mins

🍽️Yield: 4 Servings

INGREDIENTS

These are the ingredients you would need to get the best pancakes. The measurements depend on the quantity you want to make.

  • Flour🍚
  • Milk🥛
  • Eggs🥚
  • Baking powder
  • Salt🧂
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla flavor (optional)
  • Toppings (optional)🍪🍦❄️

MEASUREMENTS (for 4 servings)

  • 1.5 Cups
  • 1.25 Cups
  • 1 Large
  • 3.5 tsp
  • 1 tsp
  • 1 tbsp
  • 1 tsp
  • As desired!

RECIPE

  1. Step 1: Whisk dry ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Add wet ingredients.
  3. Step 3: Mix until mostly smooth.
  4. Step 4: Heat a buttered skillet.
  5. Step 5: Flip when bubbles form!
  6. Step 6: Serve hot with syrup.
